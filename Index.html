Discover the perfect blend of luxury, comfort, and exceptional service

Excellence in Hospitality Since 2020

One Step Hotel represents the pinnacle of luxury accommodation in Prague. Located in the historic city center, our boutique hotel offers an intimate and sophisticated experience for discerning travelers.

Each of our carefully designed rooms and suites combines modern amenities with classic elegance, ensuring your stay is both comfortable and memorable. Our dedicated team of hospitality professionals is committed to providing personalized service that exceeds your expectations.
